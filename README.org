* Episteme

#+begin_quote
Epistēmē (ἐπιστήμη) is the Greek word for knowledge or understanding: the
theoretical, reasoned grasp of how and why things are. Aristotle contrasts it
with /praxis/ (action) and /technē/ (craft).
#+end_quote

Following the spirit of this word, this collection of org-roam notes on
different subjects forms my personal map of knowledge --- the theoretical half
of a pair whose practical counterpart lives in the =praxis= repo (possessions,
houses, trips, the logistics of daily life).

The specific selection of topics and the reason behind them may vary; usually I
write about what I want to understand deeper, either the topic itself or its
connections to things in another subject.
